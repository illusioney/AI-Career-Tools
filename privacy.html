1. Introduction

Welcome to AI Career Tools ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Resume Content: Text from resumes you upload or paste for analysis
  • Job Descriptions: Job posting text you provide for matching
  • Interview Responses: Text or voice answers during practice sessions

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our features
  • Browser Information: Browser type, device type, operating system
  • Cookies: Session data stored in your browser

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide AI-powered resume analysis and scoring
  • Generate personalized cover letters
  • Create interview questions and feedback
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send service-related communications

4. Data Storage and Security

Important: We do NOT store your resume content, job descriptions, or interview responses on our servers. All data is processed in real-time and discarded after generating your results. Your information is only temporarily sent to our AI service provider (Google AI) for processing.

  • Data is transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption
  • No permanent storage of personal content
  • Browser local storage used only for user preferences

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AI: For AI-powered analysis (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Google Analytics: For usage analytics (if enabled by admin)
  • Google AdSense: For advertisements (if monetization enabled)

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site traffic and usage
  • Provide personalized content

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit some functionality.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (though we don't store personal content)
  • Clear your browser data and cookies
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Request information about data processing

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
